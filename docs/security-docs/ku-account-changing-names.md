# KU Account - Changing Your Legal and Preferred Names

## Summary

Protocols for updating legal and preferred names in the KU directory.

## Preferred Name

Can be updated via [myidentity.ku.edu](https://myidentity.ku.edu) or **Enroll & Pay**. Changes typically take 24 hours to propagate to Email and Canvas.

## Legal Name

- **Faculty and Staff:** Must contact Human Resources.
- **Students:** Must contact the Registrar's Office.
