# Qualtrics - Support

## Summary

Resources and contact information for the university's enterprise survey tool.

## Primary Support

For administrative or technical issues, contact KU Analytics, Institutional Research & Effectiveness at qualtricssupport@ku.edu.

## Technical Issues

The KU IT Help Desk can assist with login or access issues (itcsc@ku.edu).

## Training

Users are encouraged to utilize the Qualtrics Basecamp for advanced survey design tutorials and the "Check Survey Accessibility" tool.
