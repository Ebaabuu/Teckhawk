# Haskell University - Technology Training Workshops

## Summary

Information regarding the partnership and availability of technology training for students and staff at Haskell Indian Nations University.

## Workshops

KU IT offers free technology training sessions to the Haskell community, covering software packages and university-specific systems.

## Registration

Users can view and register for upcoming instructor-led sessions through the Technology Workshops Calendar.

## Custom Training

Departments at Haskell can request customized group sessions or individual consultations by emailing tie@ku.edu.
