# Student Training - Mandatory Alcohol and Sexual Violence Prevention

## Summary

Compliance information for mandatory student training modules.

## Requirements

- **AlcoholEdu:** Mandatory for all newly enrolled degree-seeking students under age 21.
- **Sexual Assault Prevention:** Mandatory for all new and continuing undergraduate and graduate students.

## Access

These modules are accessed through Canvas as a specific course tile.

## Compliance Holds

Failure to complete training will result in an enrollment hold. For hold issues, contact:

- **Sexual Assault Prevention:** sapec@ku.edu
- **AlcoholEdu:** whs-immunizations@ku.edu
