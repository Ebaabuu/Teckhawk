# Calendar - Syncing KU Account on iOS (iPhone/iPad)

## Instructions

1. Go to **Settings** > **Mail** > **Accounts**.
2. Tap **Add Account** and select **Microsoft Exchange**.
3. Enter your KU email address and a description.
4. Tap **Next** and then select **Sign In**.
5. Log in with your KU credentials and authenticate via **Duo**.
6. Ensure the **Calendars** toggle is switched to **On**.
7. Tap **Save**.
