# Microsoft Outlook - Setting Safe Senders

## Adding a Safe Sender (Desktop)

1. On the **Home** tab, click **Junk** > **Junk E-mail Options...**.
2. Go to the **Safe Senders** tab and click **Add**.
3. Type the email or domain (e.g., `@domain.com`) and click **OK**.

## In the Web App (OWA)

1. Settings > **Mail** > **Junk email**.
2. Under **Safe senders and domains**, click **+ Add**.
3. Enter the address and press **Enter**, then click **Save**.
