# Email - Accessing Account After a Password Change

## Summary

Instructions on how to regain access to your email after a password change across various platforms.

## Android

If using Gmail as the default, you will see an alert to update credentials. Click **Sign-in** to open the KU single sign-on (SSO) and enter your new password.

## iOS (iPhone/iPad)

1. Tap **Edit Settings** when prompted.
2. If not prompted, go to **Settings** > **Mail** > **Accounts** > **Exchange**.
3. Tap **Re-enter password** and login with your Online ID and new password.

## Outlook (Windows)

A KU SSO window should pop up automatically. If the status in the lower right shows "Trying to connect," look for this window to enter your new credentials.

## Outlook (Mac)

- **Newer Versions:** The SSO portal appears immediately upon opening.
- **Older Versions:** Look for a lightning bolt icon next to your account or a "Not Connected" status in the lower right. Click it to trigger the SSO window.

## Mac Mail

Click the notification in the upper right or the lightning bolt icon next to your KU mailbox to enter the new password in **System Settings**.
