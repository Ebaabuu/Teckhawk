# Email - Creating and Publishing an Alias

## Important Notes

- An alias does **not** change your KU Online ID.
- It can take 30 minutes to 2 hours for an alias to become active.
- You must create an alias before you can set it as your primary address.

## Creating a New Alias

1. Log in to **myidentity.ku.edu** with your KU Online ID and password.
2. Click **Email Services** > **Manage Aliases**.
3. Click **Add an Alias**.
4. Enter your desired alias and press **Add Alias**.

## Publishing/Changing Your Primary Address

1. Navigate to **myidentity.ku.edu**.
2. Select **Email Services** > **Update Your Primary Address**.
3. Select **Change** and choose the alias you wish to set as your Primary Email Alias.
