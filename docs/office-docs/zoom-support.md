# Zoom - Support

## Troubleshooting Calendar Disconnection

If your calendar stops syncing with Zoom:

1. Log in to kansas.zoom.us.
2. Go to **Profile** > **Other** (at the bottom).
3. Click **Delete** next to the calendar integration.
4. Restart the Zoom app and click **Re-connect to calendar** under the same Profile section to re-authorize.
