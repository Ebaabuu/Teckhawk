# Email - Unable to Email Specific Recipients

## Problem

You receive a delivery failure message: *"The email address you entered couldn't be found."* This often occurs due to an outdated or corrupted "suggested contact" (Autocomplete) entry in Outlook.

## How to Fix (Per-Email Basis)

1. Open a **New Email**.
2. Type the first few characters of the recipient's name or email.
3. Hover over the suggested entry in the dropdown list until it is highlighted.
4. Click the **X** button to the right of the name to remove that specific suggestion.
5. Manually type the full, correct email address.

**Note:** To remove a suggestion permanently, you must delete every reference to that address (emails, meetings, etc.) within your Office 365 account.
