# Email - Policies, Restrictions, and Limitations

## Message Limits

- **Max Message Size:** 50 MB total (including text, images, and attachments). It is recommended to keep attachments under 49 MB.
- **Daily Send Limit (Students):** 500 emails per day.
- **Recipient Limit:** Recommended maximum of 299 email addresses per message.

## Retention Policy

- **Deleted Items & Junk Folders:** Items are automatically removed after **30 days**.
- **Recovery:** Items removed by this policy can be recovered for up to **14 days** using the "Recover Deleted Items" feature. After this, they are permanently deleted.
