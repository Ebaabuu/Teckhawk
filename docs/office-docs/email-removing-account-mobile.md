# Email - Removing an Account from a Phone or Tablet

## Android (Native/Gmail App)

1. Open **Settings** > **Accounts & Backup** > **Manage accounts**.
2. Tap your KU account (Exchange or IMAP).
3. Select **Remove Account** and confirm.

## iOS (Native Mail App)

1. Open **Settings** > **Mail** > **Accounts**.
2. Select the account (usually labeled "Exchange").
3. Tap **Delete Account** at the bottom and confirm.

## Outlook App (Android/iOS)

1. Open Outlook and tap your **initials/avatar** in the top left.
2. Tap the **Gear icon** (Settings).
3. Tap the KU email address under **Accounts**.
4. Tap **Remove Account** and confirm "Delete from this device."
