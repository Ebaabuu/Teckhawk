# Email - Handling and Reporting Suspicious Mail

## Definitions

- **Phishing:** Fraudulent emails attempting to steal personal data.
- **Junk/Spam:** Unwanted bulk commercial messages.

## How to Report

- **Outlook (Windows/Mac/Web):** Select the suspicious email (view only) and click the **Report** button at the top. Choose **Phishing** or **Junk**.
- **Android/iOS:** Tap the three dots in the top right of the email and select **Report Junk**, then specify the type.
- **Third-Party Clients (Thunderbird, etc.):** Forward the suspicious email as an attachment to **abuse@ku.edu**.

## Blocking Senders

### Web App (OWA)

1. Settings (gear icon) > **Mail** > **Junk Email** > **Blocked senders and domains**.
2. Add a specific email (`name@domain.com`) or an entire domain (`@domain.com`).
