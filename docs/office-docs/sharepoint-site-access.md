# Microsoft SharePoint - Site Access

## Summary

Managing who can see or edit your SharePoint content.

## Permission Levels

- **Owners:** Full control over settings, permissions, and content.
- **Members:** Can add, edit, and delete files but cannot change site settings.
- **Visitors:** Read-only access to documents and pages.

## Adding Users

1. Go to your SharePoint site and click the **Settings (gear icon)**.
2. Select **Site permissions**.
3. Click **Add members** and type the name or email of the person you wish to invite.
