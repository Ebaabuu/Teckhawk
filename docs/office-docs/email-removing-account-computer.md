# Email - Removing an Account from a Laptop or Desktop Computer

## Mac Mail

1. Click the **Apple icon** > **System Settings** (or System Preferences).
2. Select **Internet Accounts**.
3. Choose your KU account from the list.
4. Click **Delete Account...** and confirm with **OK**.

## Outlook (Mac)

1. Open Outlook and go to **Tools** > **Accounts**.
2. Select your KU email and click the **minus icon (-)** in the lower left.
3. Click **Delete** to confirm. (Removal time depends on mailbox size).

## Outlook (Windows)

1. Go to **File** > **Account Settings** > **Account Settings**.
2. **Important:** Go to the **Data Files** tab. If your KU account is the default data file, you must click **Add** to create a new local data file, select it, and click **Set as Default** before you can remove the account.
3. Go back to the **Email** tab.
4. Select your KU email and click **Remove**.
