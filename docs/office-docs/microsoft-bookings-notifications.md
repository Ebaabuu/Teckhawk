# Microsoft Bookings - Email Notifications

## Problem

You are receiving excessive email notifications whenever an edit is made to an Office hours listing.

## How to Fix

1. Log in to [outlook.office.com/bookwithme/me](https://outlook.office.com/bookwithme/me).
2. Click on the specific **Office hours tile** (or the relevant Bookings entry).
3. Click the **Advanced Options** button.
4. Locate the **Email reminders** and **Email follow-up** section.
5. Click the **Trash Can icon** to the right of the unwanted notifications.
6. Click **Save and share** in the top right to apply the changes.
