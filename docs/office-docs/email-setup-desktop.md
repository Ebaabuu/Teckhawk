# Email - Setup and Access on Desktops and Laptops

**Note:** New employees/students cannot access the desktop Office suite until their official start date. Use the **Outlook Web App (OWA)** until then.

## Outlook Web App (OWA)

1. Visit `https://outlook.office.com`.
2. Sign in with `primaryEmail@ku.edu`, then use your **Online ID** (not email) on the KU SSO page.

## Mac Mail

1. Open Mail > **Microsoft Exchange**.
2. Enter your Name and `primaryEmail@ku.edu`.
3. Select **Sign In** and log in via KU SSO.

## Outlook (Windows/Mac)

1. Open Outlook.
2. Enter `primaryEmail@ku.edu` when prompted (or go to **File** > **Add Account**).
3. Outlook should automatically find the settings. Log in via KU SSO and Duo when the window appears.
