# Shared Spaces - Reserving a Room, Conference Room, or Space

## Summary

Instructions for booking physical locations on the KU campus through Outlook.

## Booking Process

1. Open your **Outlook Calendar**.
2. Create a **New Meeting**.
3. Click the **Room Finder** button (or **Add Rooms**).
4. Filter by building or capacity.
5. Select the room and click **OK**.
6. **Note:** Some rooms are "Auto-Accept," while others require approval from a departmental moderator. You will receive an email confirmation once the room is secured.
