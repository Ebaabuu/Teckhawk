# Microsoft Teams - Meetings

## Starting a Meeting

- **Instant Meeting:** In a Chat or Channel, click the **Meet** button (camera icon) in the top right.
- **Scheduled Meeting:** Go to the **Calendar** tab in Teams, click **New meeting**, add attendees, and click **Send**.

## In-Meeting Tools

- **Share Screen:** Use the arrow icon to share your desktop or specific windows.
- **Breakout Rooms:** (Organizers only) Split attendees into smaller groups.
- **Recording:** Click **More (...)** > **Record and transcribe** to save the session to Stream/SharePoint.
