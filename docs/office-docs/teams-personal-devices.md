# Microsoft Teams - Accessing on Personal Devices

## Summary

You can access Microsoft Teams on any personal computer, smartphone, or tablet using your KU credentials.

## Instructions

- **Web Access:** Go to [teams.microsoft.com](https://teams.microsoft.com) and log in with your `primaryEmail@ku.edu`.
- **Mobile App:** Download the **Microsoft Teams** app from the Apple App Store or Google Play Store.
- **Desktop App:** Log in to [office.com](https://office.com), click **Install Apps**, and select **Microsoft 365 apps** to include Teams.
