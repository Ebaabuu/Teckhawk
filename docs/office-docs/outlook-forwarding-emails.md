# Microsoft Outlook - Forwarding Emails

## Summary

You can set your KU email to automatically forward all incoming messages to another address.

## Instructions (via Outlook Web App - OWA)

1. Sign in at [outlook.office.com](https://outlook.office.com).
2. Click the **Settings (gear icon)** > **Mail** > **Forwarding**.
3. Toggle on **Enable forwarding**.
4. Enter the email address where you want your mail sent.
5. *Optional:* Check **Keep a copy of forwarded messages** if you want them to remain in your KU inbox as well.
6. Click **Save**.
