# Student Housing Internet Services

For detailed information on all KU Student Housing buildings, visit the KU Housing website.

## Residence & Scholarship Halls, Jayhawker Towers, and Stauffer Apartments

- **Internet Provider:** Boldyn
- **Support Contact:** 855-643-2149
- **Common Issues:** No internet service, intermittent connectivity

### Support Notes

Boldyn handles all internet-related issues in these specific buildings.

**Note:** KU IT does not support Jayhawk services in these locations, as they are reserved for KU employees and KU-purchased devices.

## Sunflower and Hawker Apartments

- **Internet & CATV Provider:** Midco
- **Support Contact:** 1-800-888-1300
- **Common Issues:** No internet, missing channels, broken jacks, or intermittent service

### Support Notes

Midco handles all internet and CATV issues for these apartments.

## Naismith Hall

**Support:** Student inquiries should be directed to KU Student Housing Leadership (e.g., contacting an RA).

## Services Available by Building

The availability of wired (Ethernet) and wireless connections varies by building. Note: This resource is provided by Apogee and may not be 100% accurate.

## Student Housing Wi-Fi Support

The Jayhawk network provided in academic and administrative buildings is separate from the network found in Student Housing. For assistance with student housing internet services, contact myResnet:

- **Phone:** 855-643-2149
- **Email:** support@myresnet.com
