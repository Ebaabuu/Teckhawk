# KU Card - Photo Upload

Troubleshooting for online KU Card photo submissions.

## Photo Requirements

Photos must be in **JPEG** format.

## iPhone/HEIF Issues

iPhone photos using HEIF format are incompatible with the upload system.

**Fix for iPhone users:**
1. Go to **Settings > Camera > Formats**
2. Select **Most Compatible**
3. Retake the photo

## Recommendations

Uploading via a computer is recommended over mobile devices for best results.
