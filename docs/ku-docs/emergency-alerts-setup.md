# Emergency Alerts - Setup and Sign Up Information

Provides instructions for KU Lawrence employees and students to opt-in or manage emergency alert text messages.

## Quick Actions

- **Opt-in (Text):** Send **START** to **226787** or **67283**
- **Opt-out (Text):** Send **STOP** to **226787** or **67283**

## Enroll & Pay Setup

1. Log into [Enroll & Pay](https://sa.ku.edu)
2. Select the **Personal Details** tile > **Contact Details**
3. Click the **+** icon under **Phone** to add your mobile number

## Troubleshooting

If you are not receiving texts despite a correct number in Enroll & Pay, text **STOP** and then **START** to the numbers above.
