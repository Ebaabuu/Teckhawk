# KU Account - Graduating, Retiring, and Leaving KU

Information on data termination and migration when leaving the university.

## Email Migration

To move KU Email data to a personal Gmail account:
1. Use Outlook's "Add Account" feature to add your personal email
2. Drag and drop emails and folders from your KU account to your personal account

## Canvas Data

Canvas data is kept for 5 years. Users are encouraged to back up files before leaving.

## Contacts Migration

To move contacts from your KU account to a personal account:
1. Open Outlook
2. Click the Contacts icon
3. Export or drag contacts to your personal account
