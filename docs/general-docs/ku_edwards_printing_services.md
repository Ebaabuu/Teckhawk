# KU Edwards Campus Printing and Copying Guide

This document outlines the printing services, costs, and locations available to students, faculty, and visitors at the KU Edwards Campus.

## Printing Locations at Edwards Campus
Printing, scanning, and copying are available using Multi-Function Devices (MFDs).
* **BEST Open Computing Lab:** Student and Express printing available.
* **Regents Center Hawks Nest (Room 120):** Student and Express printing available.
* **Regents Center 120A (Faculty and Staff Only):** Mail and copy room for faculty and staff. Requires a KU Employee ID Card to access and an access code from your department.

## Costs and Free Printing Allocation
* **Black and White Printing and Copying:** $0.08 per page.
* **Color Printing and Copying:** $0.48 per page.
* **Scanning:** Free. Scans can be sent directly to your KU email or saved to a USB thumb drive.

### Free Student Printing Funds
* **Fall and Spring Semesters:** Currently enrolled students receive $8.00 in free printing funds.
* **Summer Semester:** Students receive $4.00 in free printing funds.
* **Expiration:** Funds are deposited the week classes begin and are removed one week after finals. Unused free funds do not roll over to the next semester.

## How to Print (PaperCut System)
KU uses the PaperCut Print System, which is directly tied to your KU Card and Beak 'Em Bucks account.

**Standard Printing:**
1. Log in to a campus computer using your KU Online ID and password.
2. A small window will appear in the top-right corner displaying your available print balance.
3. Select Print on your document and choose the default location printer.
4. A confirmation window will show the total cost. Click Print to release the job.
5. **Important Security Note:** You must log off the computer when you are finished so others cannot print using your account.

**Express Printing:**
Walk-up Express Print stations allow you to print from a USB drive, email attachment, or web storage without logging fully into a workstation. 
* Sessions are limited to 5 minutes. 
* You will be prompted to enter your KU Online ID and password on the printer screen.

## How to Add Money for Printing
Once your free printing funds are depleted, print jobs are charged to your Beak 'Em Bucks account. 
1. Log into your **myKU Student Portal** (my.ku.edu).
2. Navigate to the **Finances** tab, then select **My Finances**.
3. Click **Recharge your card** or **Recharge your Beak 'Em Bucks**.
4. Enter the amount you wish to add using a credit card or bank account. 
*Note: Beak 'Em Bucks recharges have a maximum of $200 and are limited to one transaction every 7 calendar days.*

## Refunds
Refunds are only granted for physical equipment issues, such as paper jams or low toner. Refunds will not be issued for formatting errors or accidental prints.
* To request a refund, log in to the MyPrint website, locate your document in the history, and click the Refund button.
