# KU Edwards Campus Equipment Checkout and Loan Guide

This document outlines the policies, available items, and return procedures for checking out technology and equipment at the University of Kansas Edwards Campus.

## Library and IT Equipment Checkout (For Students, Faculty, and Staff)
The KU Libraries and IT desks provide various types of equipment for short-term checkout. 

**Available Equipment Generally Includes:**
* Laptops (4-hour short-term and 1-week checkouts)
* Computer accessories (headphones, mice, cables)
* Calculators
* Digital voice recorders

**Requirements:**
* A valid KU Card and current enrollment at, or employment with, the University of Kansas.
* Your account must be in good standing (borrowers who owe $80.00 or more in library charges will be blocked from checking out materials).
* Equipment is available on a first-come, first-served basis.

## Classroom Equipment Loan (For Instructors and GTAs Only)
KU IT loans specific equipment for free classroom instructional use. 
* **Note:** This equipment is checked out only to instructors and GTAs for instructional use. It is not available to students for personal use.

**Available Classroom Equipment:**
* Portable video projectors and screens
* Wireless presentation remotes
* Small portable sound systems

**How to Request:**
* Contact Classroom Technology Support at 785-864-1200 or via the KU IT online request form.
* Instructors are strongly encouraged to make equipment reservations at least 48 hours in advance. Equipment may be reserved for up to 10 business days.

## Returning Equipment
Because equipment is delicate and high-demand, strict return policies apply to avoid damage and late fees.
* **Service Desk Return:** Library equipment (including laptops, headphones, and calculators) must be returned directly to a staff member at the service desk from which the items were first checked out.
* **Do Not Use Book Drops:** Do not place laptops or electronic equipment in the walk-up book returns or the outdoor return in front of the Regents Center Building. Placing equipment in standard book returns may result in damage charges or overdue fines.
* **Due Dates:** All short-term, reserve, and equipment loan items checked out for 2 weeks or less must be returned by the exact time due.

## Fines and Overdue Charges
There is no grace period for any equipment items checked out. Each item must be returned in good condition by the exact date and time due. 
* **4-Hour Laptops:** Overdue fine of $0.10 per minute (maximum of $30 per item).
* **1-Week Laptops:** Overdue fine of $10.00 per day (maximum of $100 per item).
* **Calculators & Accessories:** Overdue fine of $1.00 per hour (maximum of $25 or $10 per item, depending on the accessory).
* **Lost Equipment:** Equipment that is 10 days overdue will be declared lost. The borrower will be billed for the replacement cost of the item, a $20 processing fee, and the maximum overdue fine.
