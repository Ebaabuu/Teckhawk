# KU Wi-Fi Access & Network Connection Guide

This document outlines the official wireless networks available at the University of Kansas (including the Edwards Campus) and the instructions for connecting to them.

## Primary Networks

### 1. JAYHAWK (For Students, Faculty, and Staff)
The **JAYHAWK** wireless network is the fast, secure, and primary network for all current KU faculty, staff, and students. 

**How to connect:**
1. Open the Wi-Fi/Network settings on your computer or mobile device.
2. Select the **JAYHAWK** network from the list of available networks.
3. When prompted, enter your **KU Online ID** (e.g., a123b456).
4. Enter your **KU Password**.
5. *Note:* If prompted to accept or trust a security certificate, click **Accept/Trust** to finalize the connection.

### 2. KUGUEST (For Campus Visitors)
The **KUGUEST** wireless service is available to all campus visitors who do not have a KU Online ID.

**How to connect:**
1. Open the Wi-Fi/Network settings on your device.
2. Select the **KUGUEST** network.
3. Open a web browser; you should be redirected to a login/acceptance portal to access the public internet.

### 3. eduroam (For Visitors from Member Institutions)
The University of Kansas is a member of **eduroam**, a global consortium offering secure Wi-Fi access across thousands of institutions.
* **Non-KU Visitors:** If you are visiting the KU Edwards campus from another eduroam-participating university, select the **eduroam** network and log in using your home institution's credentials (typically your full university email and password).
* **KU Personnel Traveling:** KU students and staff can use their KU credentials (`[KU_Online_ID]@ku.edu`) to log into the eduroam network when visiting other participating universities.

## Special Access Networks

### KU-Passport (Sponsored Campus Visitors)
The **KU-Passport** wireless service is an elevated tier for select visitors sponsored by a KU faculty or staff member. It offers a faster connection than KUGUEST because there is no shared bandwidth limit.
* **How to get access:** KU faculty and staff must request KU-Passport access for a visitor or group by contacting their departmental technology support staff, who will route the request to the KU IT Help Desk.

### Housing Wi-Fi (ResNet)
If you are in KU campus housing, internet service is provided by an external partner (Apogee/ResNet), not the standard campus IT networks. Visit the Housing Wi-Fi service page on the KU IT website for specific setup instructions.

## Troubleshooting & Support
If your device will not connect to the JAYHAWK network:
1. "Forget" the network in your device's Wi-Fi settings and attempt to reconnect.
2. Ensure your KU Password has not expired.
3. **Edwards Campus Support:** Visit Regents Center, Room 128, or call 913-897-8652.
4. **24/7 Support:** Contact the main KU IT Help Desk at 785-864-8080 or ithelpdesk@ku.edu.
