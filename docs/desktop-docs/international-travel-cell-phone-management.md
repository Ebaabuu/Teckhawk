# International Travel - Cell Phone Management Guide

## Guidelines for Departments

Departments managing mobile devices for international travelers must follow these best practices for setup and usage. Note that KU IT does not support external services or account registrations.

## General Recommendations

- **Phone Numbers:** Do not share numbers between users. This can interfere with service registration.

## Inventory Tracking

- **Labeling:** Use a label maker to print the serial and phone number. Place it on the back of the device.
- **Box Retention:** Keep the original box and label it with the same information for protection and identification.

## Device Setup

- **Automatic Enrollment:** Devices automatically enroll in KU IT's management platform during initial setup.
- **Mandatory PIN:** Users are required to set a PIN code; this step cannot be skipped.

## Duo Support

- **Temporary Registration:** Users can enroll additional devices by temporarily registering the travel device number with their Duo account.
- **Assistance:** Contact KU IT at 785-864-8080 for Duo support.

## Software Installation

- **App Store:** Disabled by default on KU IT-managed devices.
- **Pre-approved Apps:** Install approved software via the **Self Service** app on the device.
- **Additional Software:**
  - For accommodations, visit Employment Accommodations at accessibility.ku.edu.
  - For travel-specific requests, contact the **Global Risk and Security team**.
  - KU IT only processes changes after direct notification from Global Risk.

## Post-Travel Device Actions

- **Device Wipe:** All devices must be wiped after international travel.
- **PIN Update:** The user may need to reset the PIN before returning the device.
- **Erasure:** Follow official Apple Support instructions to erase the iPhone.
