# PaperCut - Public Printing

The PaperCut Print System is currently in use in public computing labs and libraries across the KU campus. It is directly tied into the KU Card system and uses computer authentication to identify who is printing.

**Important:** It is imperative that students log off computers when they leave so that others do not use their accounts to print.

## 1. Printing Information

- **Black and White:** $0.08 per page
- **Color:** $0.48 per page
- **Free Allocation:** Currently enrolled undergraduate students are supplied with $8.00 of free printing. Once used, you must have funds on your KU Card (Beak 'Em Bucks) to print.

## 2. Printing from Canvas

To avoid formatting problems when printing documents from Canvas, follow these steps:

1. Right-click the document link in Canvas.
2. Select **Save link as...** and save the document to the computer desktop.
3. Double-click the file on the desktop to open it in its original application (e.g., Word, Adobe Reader).
4. Print directly from that application instead of the web browser.

## 3. Printing Instructions

### Windows

1. **Check Balance:** Look for the small PaperCut window in the top-right corner showing your available funds.
2. **Print Document:** Click File > Print.
3. **Select Printer:** Choose the desired printer and click Print.
4. **Confirm:** A second window will list the document name, printer, page count, and total cost. If correct, press Print to release the job.
5. **Express Stations:** In Anschutz and Watson Libraries, use Express Printing stations for walk-up use. You will be asked for your Online ID and password before the job processes.

### Mac

1. **Check Balance:** Verify your funds in the top-right PaperCut window.
2. **Print Document:** Click File > Print.
3. **Authenticate:** A small window will appear with your name. Delete your name, enter your KU Online ID and Password, and click Okay.
4. **Confirm:** Review the document details and total cost in the confirmation window. Click Print to release.

## 4. Recharging Your KU Card

To add money for printing, you can recharge your card through:

- **Online:** myKU portal
- **Physical Locations:** Cash-to-card machines in Anschutz Library or the Kansas Union
