# Web Browser - Troubleshooting Tips for Mobile Devices

Many KU web services use pop-up windows to display information. Depending on the service or the browser, you may have to allow for pop-ups in order for the information to come up. Common KU services that use pop-ups include Enroll & Pay, HR/Pay, myKU, MyTalent, and the Student Housing app.

## 1. Allow Pop-up Windows

To allow pop-ups for the KU domain in your mobile browser, you typically need to adjust the settings within the specific browser app you are using.

**Action:** Locate the Settings menu in your mobile browser and look for "Pop-ups" or "Content Settings".

**Recommendation:** Set the browser to always allow pop-ups from ku.edu.

## 2. Clear Cache & Cookies

At times, cached files can cause errors on websites, including log-in issues and display problems. As a first step in resolving most website issues, KU IT recommends clearing the browser cache and cookies to see if that's the cause of the problem.

**How to:** Access your browser's History or Privacy settings to find the option to "Clear Browsing Data".

**Tip:** After clearing, close all browser tabs and restart the app.

## 3. Enable Third-Party Cookies

Systems such as Canvas and Microsoft Integration with OneDrive require you to allow Third-party Cookies.

**Setup:** Go to your mobile browser's Privacy or Security settings and ensure that "Block all cookies" or "Block third-party cookies" is disabled.

## 4. Open a Private Browsing Window

There are times when it is necessary to open a private browsing window, either to circumvent logged in accounts (without having to log out of them) or to prevent a website from accessing the browser cache.

**Access:** Look for the Tabs icon in your mobile browser and select Private, Incognito, or InPrivate mode.

## 5. Viewing Saved Credentials

If you are able to access an account with a saved credential, but unable to log in with a typed password, you may be able to confirm the password you're typing against the saved credential on your device.

**Location:** Check your mobile device's Passwords section (found in system settings for iOS or browser settings for Android/Chrome).

**Note:** A saved credential is not always or necessarily a correct one, and viewing it is no guarantee of access to an account.
