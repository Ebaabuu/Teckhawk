# MathWorks MatLab - Access and Installation

## Summary

Instructions for accessing and installing MatLab. Note that steps may vary slightly depending on your activation method.

## Installation Steps

1. **Account Setup:** Go to [MathWorks Login](https://www.mathworks.com/login) and log in or create an account using your **KU or KUMC email address**.
2. **License Assignment:** Once verified, a license is assigned automatically.
3. **Download:** Click the **Download icon**, select your release and Operating System.
4. **Installer:** Open the installer and log in.
5. **Agreement:** Accept the license agreement and confirm your user profile.
6. **Configuration:** Select the destination folder, choose your desired products/toolboxes, and set your options.
7. **Execution:** Click **Begin Install** and wait for completion.

## Adding Toolboxes

If MatLab is already installed and you need to add toolboxes, do not reinstall MatLab itself. You can reuse the original installer or download it again (ensure it is the same release version as your current installation).

1. Follow the Installation steps above from Step 1 through Step 6.
2. At the product selection step, **de-select MatLab** and only select the desired toolbox add-ons.
3. Continue with the remaining installation steps.
