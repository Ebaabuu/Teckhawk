# KU Anywhere (VPN) - Setup

## Summary

The Cisco Secure Client (formerly AnyConnect) provides a secure, remote connection for faculty and staff to access campus systems while working off-campus. This software is available for all major operating systems, including mobile devices.

## Step 1: Setting Entitlements (Required)

Before you can connect, you must authorize your account for VPN access through the MyIdentity portal.

1. Visit [myidentity.ku.edu](https://myidentity.ku.edu) in your web browser.
2. Log in with your **KU Online ID** and password.
3. Click **Learn More** under the **Other Services** section.
4. Choose **KU Anywhere (VPN Service at KU)**.
   - *Note: If this option is missing, your account may not be entitled for access. Contact IT at 785-864-8080.*
5. Select the specific **VPN entitlement** required for your needs (e.g., Default or Departmental).
6. Click **Submit**.
7. **Wait Time:** It takes approximately **1-2 minutes** for entitlements to become active.

## Step 2: Software Installation & Initial Connection Setup

### Windows & macOS (Non-Mobile)

1. **Download:** Access the [KU Software Web Store](https://kusoftware.ku.edu) to download the Cisco Secure Client installer.
2. **Install:** Run the installer and follow the setup wizard prompts.
3. **Launch:** Open the **Cisco Secure Client** from your Start menu (Windows) or Applications folder (Mac).
4. **Server Address:** In the connection box, enter: `kuanywhere.ku.edu`.
5. **Connect:** Click **Connect**.
6. **Group Selection:** Change the Group from "Default" to **KU-Single-Signon**.
7. **Login:** Authenticate with your KU Online ID, password, and Duo MFA.

### Mobile Devices (Android & iOS)

1. **Download:** Find **Cisco Secure Client** (or Cisco AnyConnect) in the Google Play Store or Apple App Store.
2. **Setup Connection:**
   - Open the app and select **Connections** > **Add VPN Connection**.
   - **Description:** KU Anywhere
   - **Server Address:** `kuanywhere.ku.edu`
3. **Connect:** Toggle the VPN to **On** and log in via the Single Sign-On page.

## Services that Require VPN Access

You do **not** need the VPN while on campus. Only use it when off-campus to access the following:

- **File Storage:** Accessing your **G: drive** (Central File Storage) or Research File Storage.
- **Departmental Resources:** Local departmental file servers or databases.
- **CMS Access:** Managing sites through the KU CMS Sunflower platform.
- **HPC Clusters:** Connecting to Community Research Computing (CRC) clusters.

> **Tip:** Disconnect from the VPN when you are finished accessing these specific resources to optimize your internet speed.
