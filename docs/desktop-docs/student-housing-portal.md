# Student Housing Portal - FAQ

## Browser Compatibility and Payment Issues

- **Browser Recommendation:** You may use any browser to access the portal.
- **Safari Users:** If you have cookies disabled in Safari, you will be unable to submit payments.
- **Resolution:** Enable third-party cookies or switch to a different browser.

## 1. Signing Into the Portal

1. Navigate to the myKU portal.
2. Log in using your KU Online ID and password.
3. Click on **Services** in the top-center navigation bar.
4. Locate the Student Housing section (typically near the bottom left of the page).
5. Select **Sign in to Student Housing**.

## 2. Troubleshooting Access

If the portal site does not appear after clicking the sign-in button, it is likely being blocked by your browser.

- **Pop-up Blockers:** You must disable your pop-up blocker or add ku.edu as an exception.
- **Related Guide:** Refer to the Web Browser Troubleshooting Tips for Desktops or Mobile Devices for detailed instructions.

## 3. Portal Features and Capabilities

Students can use the portal to manage several housing-related tasks:

- **Contracts:** Sign your housing contract or scholarship hall agreement.
- **Room Selection:** Find your group number, select your room, and choose a dining plan.
- **Roommates:** Create roommate pull-in codes and find your roommates' contact information.
- **Break Housing:** Reserve housing during Thanksgiving, winter, or spring breaks.
