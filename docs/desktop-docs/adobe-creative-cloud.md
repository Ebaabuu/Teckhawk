# Adobe Creative Cloud - Detailed Usage & Troubleshooting

## Sign In Procedures

To access Adobe applications, you must sign in using your University of Kansas credentials.

### For Employees

1. Open the **Adobe Creative Cloud Desktop App** or an individual Adobe application.
2. Enter your **primary KU email address** and select **Continue**.
3. If prompted to "Select an account," choose **Company or School Account**.
4. Sign in via the KU Single Sign-On (SSO) page with your KU Online ID and password.

### For Students

- **Lab Access:** Sign in on any centrally-managed public computer lab machine using the same SSO process described above.
- **Personal Devices:** If you have purchased a license through the KU Bookstore, use your registered email to log into the Creative Cloud Desktop app.

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

If you encounter errors during installation or sign-in, follow these steps:

### "Trial Expired" or "Buy Now" Prompts

- Ensure you are signed in with your **KU email**, not a personal Adobe ID.
- Sign out and sign back in to refresh the license state.

### License Not Found (Student Employees)

- Verify you have claimed the license at [license.ku.edu](https://license.ku.edu/).
- Wait at least **2 hours** after claiming before attempting to sign in.

### Installation Errors

- **Windows:** Check the Software Center for available updates.
- **Mac:** Use the Self Service app to repair or reinstall the Creative Cloud Desktop client.

## How to Sign Out

It is recommended to sign out if you are using a shared or public computer:

1. Open the **Creative Cloud Desktop app**.
2. Select the **Account icon** in the upper right corner.
3. Click **Sign Out**.
