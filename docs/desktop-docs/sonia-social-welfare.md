# Sonia (School of Social Welfare) - Access and Information

The School of Social Welfare uses the Sonia application to track Field Education Placements. Users must select one of three roles to log in: Student, Agency, or Field Liaison, each using a specific authentication method.

## 1. Login Procedures

To begin, navigate to the Sonia portal and select School of Social Welfare.

### Student Role

- **Authentication:** Click University Sign In to be redirected to the KU Single Sign-On (SSO) page.
- **Troubleshooting:** If you receive an error message about "unable to retrieve details" or see a blank page after a successful SSO login, the issue should be escalated to the ESWJL TSC - Twente group.

### Field Liaison Role

- **Authentication:** Choose the Field Liaison role and log in with your KU Online ID and password.
- **Troubleshooting:** Verify your credentials at myidentity.ku.edu. If credentials are correct but the "unable to retrieve details" error persists, escalate the ticket to the ESWJL TSC - Twente group.

### Agency Role

- **Authentication:** Select the Agency role. Log in using your registered agency email address and the unique password you created for the system.
- **Management:** These accounts are maintained directly within the Sonia system. Any login issues should be referred to the ESWJL TSC - Twente group.

## 2. Important Notes & Support

- **Password Resets:** The "Forgot Password" link on the Sonia login page only works for the Agency role. Students and Liaisons must manage their passwords through standard KU IT channels.
- **Escalation:** For issues that cannot be resolved via standard troubleshooting, assign tickets to the ESWJL TSC - Twente group.
