# Printing and Scanning - Information and Locations

Multi-function devices (MFD) in campus computer labs and in many KU Libraries locations provide the ability for students, faculty, and staff to scan, copy, and print documents.

## Printing

### Costs and Allocation

- **Black & White:** $0.08 per page
- **Color:** $0.48 per page
- **Free Allocation:** Currently enrolled students receive $8.00 of free printing during the fall and spring semesters, and $4.00 during the summer.
- **Payment:** Use Beak 'Em Bucks on your KU Card once free funds are exhausted.

### Printing Services

- **Print from Anywhere:** Allows you to send documents from a personal computer or mobile device to campus printers via myprint.ku.edu.
- **Express Printing:** Quick-print stations for USB drives or email attachments (5-minute limit). Locations include Anschutz Library, Watson Library, Murphy Art & Architecture Library, and Spahr Engineering Library.
- **Public Computers:** Sign in with your KU Online ID. After selecting "Print," a pop-up will show the cost and page count. Confirm to send the job to the queue.

### Releasing Your Job

1. Go to any MFD in the building where you sent the job.
2. Log in by swiping your KU Card or entering your Online ID and password.
3. Touch the **Release** button, select your documents, and press the blue **Start** button.
4. Press the **Access/Logout** button when finished to secure your account.

## Scanning

### General Information

- **Cost:** Scanning is free for all users.
- **Requirements:** You must log in to the MFD with your KU Online ID or KU Card.

### Locations and Equipment

- **Library Locations:** Scanning is available at Watson, Anschutz, Music & Dance, Murphy Art & Architecture, and Spahr Engineering libraries.
- **Scanner Types:**
  - **Bookeye Overhead Scanners:** Best for books and fragile items.
  - **MFDs:** Standard scanners that support multi-page document feeding.

### How to Scan

1. Place your original face down on the glass or face up in the document feeder.
2. Log in to the MFD.
3. Select **Scan** or **Fax/Scan**.
4. Choose Destination:
   - **Email Me:** Sends a PDF directly to your KU email address.
   - **OneDrive:** Saves the file directly to your KU OneDrive for Business account.
   - **USB Drive:** Save directly to a portable flash drive.
5. Adjust settings (color, 2-sided, file name) and press the blue **Start** button.
