# PaperCut - Printing Funds

This guide explains how to manage your printing funds, understand your balance, and troubleshoot issues with free printing allocations at KU.

## Adding Money

In order to print after your free allocation is used, you must have funds on your KU Card.

### Recharge Online

1. Log in to the myKU portal with your Online ID and password.
2. Select Finances from the navigation bar, then click My Finances.
3. Click Recharge your card.
4. Select Recharge your Beak 'Em Bucks, enter your amount and payment method, and complete the form.

### Physical Locations

You can also use cash-to-card machines located in the Kansas Union or Anschutz Library.

## Color vs. B&W Printing Charges

Printing costs are deducted automatically when you release a job.

- **Black & White:** $0.08 per page
- **Color:** $0.48 per page

**Important:** If any color is present in a document, you will be charged the color price. To ensure you are charged for B&W, change your print settings to grayscale or black and white before sending the job.

## Free Printing Funds Not Loaded to KU Card

Currently enrolled students are provided with $8.00 of free printing during fall/spring semesters and $4.00 during summer. Funds are added the week classes begin and removed a week after finals.

Common reasons funds may not be visible:

- **Eligibility:** You must be a currently enrolled student with a valid KU Card.
- **Timing:** If you just received your card or enrolled in classes, funds will appear the following day.
- **Semester Breaks:** Free printing is only available during the active academic semester.
- **Staff/GTAs:** Graduate Teaching Assistants (GTAs) do not receive this allocation but can print for free in the Watson Library Graduate Study.

## Understanding MyPrint Printing Balance

Your printing balance is a combination of your Free Printing allocation, any Beak 'Em Bucks you've added, and any printing refunds you've received.

- **Prioritization:** The system will use your free printing funds first before pulling from your Beak 'Em Bucks.
- **Rollover:** Unused free printing funds do not roll over to the next semester and are reclaimed after finals.

## View Printing Balances

You can check your current balance in two primary ways:

- **On-Screen:** When you log in to a public campus computer (Windows or Mac), a small PaperCut window will appear in the top-right corner showing your available funds.
- **myKU Portal:**
  1. Log in to my.ku.edu.
  2. Navigate to Finances > My Finances.
  3. View the Finances section at the top left to see both your Beak 'Em Bucks and MyPrint balance.
