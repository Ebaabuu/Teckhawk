# PaperCut - Web Print FAQ

## Cost / Refund / Access

- **Free Printing Allocation:** Currently enrolled students receive $8.00 of free printing during the fall and spring semesters and $4.00 during the summer.
- **Eligibility Criteria:**
  - You must have a KU Card. If you recently received it, funds appear the following day.
  - You must be currently enrolled in classes.
  - You must have a registered KU email address (setup via myidentity.ku.edu).
- **Printing Costs:** Black & white prints are $0.08 per page, and color prints are $0.48 per page.
- **Refunds:** Refunds are generally only issued for printer malfunctions (e.g., jams, smearing) and not for user errors like typos or incorrect formatting.

## Print Anywhere (PaperCutMF)

Web Print is the recommended method for printing from personal laptops to KU public lab and multifunction printers (MFDs).

- **Access Portal:** Log in at myprint.ku.edu using your KU Online ID.
- **Supported File Types:** Microsoft Excel, Word, PowerPoint, XPS, and PDF.
- **Limitations:**
  - **Single-sided only:** Duplex printing is currently unavailable via Web Print.
  - **File Size:** Jobs cannot exceed 100MB.
  - **Queue Time:** Jobs are held in the queue for 2 hours before being deleted.
  - **Full Document:** You must print the entire document; specific page selection is not supported.

## Using a Printer

### 1. Submitting a Job

1. Log into myprint.ku.edu and select **Web Print** from the left menu.
2. Click **Submit a Job**.
3. Select the appropriate building queue (e.g., Anschutz Library (virtual)).
4. Click **Print Options and Account Selection**, enter the number of copies, and click **Upload Documents**.
5. Upload your file and click **Upload & Complete**. The status will change to "Held in a queue".

### 2. Releasing the Print Job at an MFD

1. Walk up to any MFD and authenticate by swiping your KU Card or manually entering your Online ID and password.
2. Touch the **Release** tab or button on the screen.
3. Select your document(s) and press the blue **Start** button to begin printing.

**Important:** Press the Access/Logout button when finished to prevent others from using your funds.
