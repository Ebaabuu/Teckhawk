# Microsoft Remote Help - Getting Help from IT Support

## Summary

This guide outlines the process for receiving technical assistance via Microsoft Remote Help and describes the prompts you will see when an IT support technician initiates a connection to your device.

## Receiving Help with Microsoft Remote Help

Once you have requested assistance from IT Support, a sign-in window will appear on your device.

1. **Sign In:** Click the Sign in button.
2. **Authenticate:** Select your Microsoft work or school account and enter your KU Online ID (e.g., j123h456@ku.edu).
3. **Connection Messages:** The IT support technician will launch the session. You will see notifications at the middle and bottom of your screen indicating the Remote Assistance Session is starting. Note: You do not need to click these specific messages.
4. **Allow Connection:** When the notification to allow or decline the connection appears, click **Allow** to begin the session.
5. **Heads-Up-Display (HUD):** A support HUD will appear at the top of your screen once the technician is connected. They will now be able to view and control your screen to provide assistance.
6. **Session End:** When the session concludes, you will receive a confirmation message. You may then close all open support windows and return to normal use of your device.
